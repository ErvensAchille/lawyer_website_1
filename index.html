Learn About Us

Founding Attorney, Joe Fouché III is a Civil Rights litigator and advocate for Real People that were harmed by Excessive Force (42 U.S. Code § 1983 cases), personal injuries from negligence, or the criminal justice system. Here at the Law Offices of Joe Fouché Esquire, our approach is client-focused and based on the facts, not opinions. Leading each case with the facts at the forefront and the client’s best interests driving us, ensures that each case is constructed thoroughly, not only for success in litigation, but to maximize negotiation outcomes for the best possible resolutions for our clients. We also provide legal and business services by helping entrepreneurs and small businesses navigate the hurdles of owning a business regarding contract formation, enforcement, compliance, and dispute negotiation.

With a diverse background including project management, and public affairs, Attorney Fouché uses Real-World experiences with Real People to get Real Results. Here at the Law Offices, we integrate modern solutions and data-driven litigation strategies to find the right team for a given case.

At the Law Offices of Joe Fouché Esquire, We Fight for Full Justice.
